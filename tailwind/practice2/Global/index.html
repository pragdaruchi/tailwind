TWO BRANDS, WEAVING
FORWARD TOGETHER

Welcome to Something Good Studio, the new
home to both Something Good Homeware and
Romaria Knitwear. Founded by Zydia Botes,
Something Good Studio has conceptualised and
brought to life stand-out textile pieces in
collaboration with artists and designers since
2018. In 2019, Zydia joined forces with Carla Pinto
to produce Romaria, a fashion and lifestyle
knitwear collective where luxury meets comfort.
Now, these two kindred and dynamic textile
design brands can be found here, in one place.

WE COLLABORATE WITH
ARTISTS & DESIGNERS

We are a collaborative studio, conceptualising
and bringing to life stand-out textile pieces, from
knitwear to design and collectable art. We work
together with artists and designers whose work
primarily portray a visual language of playfulness.
Recent collaborators include designer
TheUrbanative and artists Lulama Wolf, Maja
Maljević, Renée Rossouw and Ditiro Mashigo.

WE WORK ON CUSTOM
PROJECTS

We provide a customised design service for
special projects and occasions. We work with
various textile manufactures, craftsmen in South
Africa to create standout textile pieces, making
use of our relationships and knowledge acquired
over time to approach new projects in the most
professional and efficient manner. Some of our
clients include luxury 5 star hotel, Kruger Shalati,
and art galleries such as Goodman Gallery and
Berman Contemporary.

WE'RE WEAVING FORWARD
TOGETHER

In community, we use art and design to make a
positive impact in the lives of our clients and
collaborators, and in the process bring awareness
to remarkable craftsmanship. Our textile pieces –
while maintaining the European and South African
influence of our joint heritage – are proudly made
in South Africa of locally sourced natural fibers
and wonderful quality to be enjoyed throughout
all seasons.

SLOWLY, WELL MADE IN
SOUTH AFRICA

Our textile pieces are made slowly and well on a
small scale in South Africa, and consist of natural
fibres and remarkable stitch games. We don't
chase seasons or trends. We celebrate timeless
quality to be enjoyed throughout all seasons.
